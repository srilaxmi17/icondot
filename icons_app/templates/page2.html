Frequently Asked Questions

Can I download any resource after becoming a member?

Yes, Subscription to Iconsdot grants you full access to our entire library of icons, including any new icons added during your subscription period.

What are the payment options?

Iconsdot accepts a variety of payment methods, including major credit and debit cards, through our secure payment gateway. We don't capture any of your credit card details.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription by logging into your Iconsdot account, navigating to the “Profile > Subscription" section, and selecting the option to cancel your subscription.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, you will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing cycle. After that, your account will revert to the free tier.

I’m not satisfied with your resources. Can I have my money back?

Yes, membership subscriptions are refundable only within the first week if fewer than 5 icons have been downloaded. You cannot use the downloaded resources for commercial projects if you request a refund.

Can I hire your team for custom design work?

Yes, Iconsdot offers custom design services. If you need unique icons or specific design work, you can contact our team through the "Custom Design form" section on our website. Provide details about your project, and we will get back to you with a quote and timeline.
